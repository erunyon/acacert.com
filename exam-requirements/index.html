--- layout: default title: "Examination Requirements" ---

Eligibility to sit for each exam requires at least a high school diploma or GED plus the individual minimum requirements listed.

Applications must be completed, signed and received at least 10 days before the scheduled examination date. The examination fee must accompany the application or it will not be processed. Each person must meet and provide supporting documentation of the stated minimum requirements before the application will be processed. All applications are subject to content verification and approval. Ineligible applicants will be refunded the examination fee minus a $35.00 processing fee. No refunds will be made for no-shows on the exam date.

Examination study guides are provided to approved applicants.
