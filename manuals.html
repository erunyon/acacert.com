Phlebotomy Training & Review Manual (2021) 90+ pgs.

This manual is consistent with current CLSI guidelines and NAACLS phlebotomy competencies. This manual can be used to teach a basic phlebotomy course or as a review for any national certification exam or general phlebotomy review. A 100 question practice exam comes with each manual.

Electrocardiography Training & Review Manual (2021) 90+ pgs.

This manual can be used to teach a basic ECG course or for someone who wants to learn the basics of ECG on their own. This manual provides a review for any national ECG certification exam. A 100 question practice exam comes with each manual.
